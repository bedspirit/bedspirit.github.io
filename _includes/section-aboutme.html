Collaboration in a team environment

Midway through the computer science program at SNHU, I was surprised to find that many of the courses did not focus on coding, but on the way that software development teams functioned. These were not my favorite courses because I really wanted to code, but I did learn alot about the structure, process, and collaborative nature that teams employ. I am confident that I can seamlessly collaborate with members of diverse teams.
